# Design Guidelines — Crustyverr

## Colors
- Primary: Warm brown (#6B3F1F) 
- Accent: Cream/latte (#F5E6C8)
- Background: Off-white (#FAFAF7)
- Text: Dark espresso (#1C1007)

## Typography
- Headings: Serif or display font, warm and inviting
- Body: Clean sans-serif, readable
- Tone: Cozy, premium home feel

## Style
- Minimal, clean layout
- Large hero with coffee imagery/gradient
- Card-based product section
- Sticky or inline CTA button
- Mobile-first responsive

## Components
- Hero section with headline + subheadline + CTA button
- Product cards (3-4 items) with name, description, price placeholder
- Contact/order section with WhatsApp button or form
